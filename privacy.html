Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FlowDesk ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use FlowDesk at kolimoli1.github.io.

Information we collect

When you create an account or use FlowDesk, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address (used for account creation)
  • Tasks, habits, courses, and study preferences you enter into the app
  • Pomodoro session data (focus time, session counts)
  • Google Calendar events (only if you choose to connect your Google Calendar — read-only access)
  • Theme and display preferences stored in your browser's localStorage

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the FlowDesk service
  • Sync your tasks, habits, and study data across sessions
  • Display your Google Calendar events alongside your FlowDesk tasks (if connected)
  • Remember your display preferences (theme, color, font)

Google Calendar data

If you choose to connect Google Calendar, FlowDesk requests read-only access to your primary calendar. We only read event titles and times to display them in the Weekly Planner. We do not store, share, or modify your Google Calendar data. You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the Planner view.

Data storage

Your account data (tasks, habits, courses, profiles) is stored securely using Supabase, which provides row-level security so only you can access your own data. Display preferences are stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not display advertisements or allow advertisers to access your data.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@flowdeskapp.com. We will permanently delete your data within 30 days of your request.

Cookies and local storage

FlowDesk uses browser localStorage to remember your theme preferences and Google Calendar access token. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Children's privacy

FlowDesk is designed for students and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact us

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at hello@flowdeskapp.com
